Sources for every statistic and market claim in [STRATEGY.md](STRATEGY.md). Compiled 2026-07-14 via web research.

**Verification key:**
- **[V]** — Verified: confirmed in the primary source or directly quoted from search-indexed source text.
- **[PV]** — Partially verified: figure retrieved via search-engine rendering of the named source; the source page itself (StatCan / canada.ca / gnb.ca) blocked direct fetching from the research environment. URLs are correct for citation; spot-check exact figures on the live page before publishing them anywhere formal (grant applications, press).
