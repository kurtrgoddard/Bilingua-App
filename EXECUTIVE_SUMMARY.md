## What is Bilingua.app?

**Bilingua.app** is a language exchange platform designed specifically for New Brunswick's bilingual community. We connect English and French speakers for language practice through in-person meetups at local coffee shops.

**Tagline**: *"Connect. Practice. Grow. - New Brunswick's Bilingual Community"*

---

## The Problem

1. **Language learners lack local practice partners** - Apps like Duolingo teach vocabulary, but don't provide real conversation practice
2. **Existing language exchange apps are global** - Tandem and HelloTalk connect you with people worldwide, but don't help you find local partners
3. **Making local connections is hard** - No easy way to find language exchange partners in your city
4. **Safety concerns** - Meeting strangers from the internet is intimidating

---

## Our Solution

Bilingua.app solves these problems by:

1. **Local-first matching** - Connect only with people in your New Brunswick city
2. **Public location focus** - Encourage meetups at coffee shops (safer, more comfortable)
3. **Cultural integration** - Celebrate Acadian, Indigenous, and Maritime cultures
4. **Safety-first design** - Built-in safety guidelines, reporting, and moderation
5. **Bilingual by design** - Full English/French support from day one
6. **Accessibility-first** - WCAG AA compliant for users with disabilities

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Advantage |
|------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| **Tandem** | Large user base, chat features | Global (not local), no meetup focus | We're local-only |
| **HelloTalk** | Translation features, gamification | Global, Asian-focused | NB cultural focus |
| **Meetup.com** | Event organizing, established | Not language-specific, event-focused | Purpose-built for language exchange |
| **Facebook Groups** | Free, familiar | Unstructured, no matching | Algorithm-based matching |
| **Language schools** | Professional teachers | Expensive ($500-2,000), formal | Free, peer-to-peer |

**Key Differentiator**: We're the only platform purpose-built for **local, in-person** language exchange in New Brunswick.
